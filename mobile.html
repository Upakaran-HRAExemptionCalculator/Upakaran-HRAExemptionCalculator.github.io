** HRA Exemption claiming tips **

1. The House Rent Allowance component of salary must not exceed 60% of Basic component of salary.

2. HRA Exemption in Income Tax can only be claimed by individuals who are residing in rented accommodation.

3. In order to claim HRA exemption one needs to submit House Rent Agreement/Rent Receipt to his or her employer.

4. If yearly rent exceeds Rs 1 Lac (Rs 8333.33 per month), landlord's PAN number must be submitted to the employer.

5. To gain maximum benefit of HRA exemption, declare high amount of HRA component in case of flexible benefit salary plans.

6. To claim HRA Exemption, the actual rent paid to landlord must not be less than 10% of summation of Basic and Dearness Allowance.
